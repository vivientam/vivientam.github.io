My Background

My Education

I was educated at Primary and Secondary Schools in Hong Kong. I was greatully taught by my teachers, who encouraged me to be creative and nice to people.

After Form 5, I was trained to be a Secretary and worked for about 5 years.

After 5 years working experience, I decided to go to Sydney to study design. I studied a Foundation course and the teacher said that I am talented at color and graphic design. But I chosed to study Industrial Design, because I wanted to help my father after I graduated. The choice does not go wrong, because I love my lectures, they taught and inspired me a lot, expecially the subject - creative technology. It inspired me to self-learn science. I love to read the magazine - New scirentist.
